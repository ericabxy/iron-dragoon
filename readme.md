# Iron Dragoon

A sprite-based retro space shooter.

Five hundred years after the rise of the Roman Republic, many men spend their lives sailing the Sea of Stars in search of wonders and bounty. You are a veteran of the Solar-Selenian War, and owner-pilot of a state-of-the-art Dragoon starship. With this iron machine you ply your trade as an aetherial explorer and porter.

## Starting Up

Make sure you have installed the [Lutro](https://lutro.libretro.com/) core for your [LibRetro](https://www.libretro.om/) front-end and load it by selecting "Load Core". Then select "Load Content" and navigate to the location of "IronDragoon.lutro".

If you have the development/unreleased version of _Iron Dragoon_, the game may be played from the source files by running the following command, assuming you have installed Lutro in the same parent folder.

    retroarch -L ../libretro-lutro/lutro_libretro.so .

## Playing the Game

Using a RetroPad assigned into Controller Port 1, press Left and Right to rotate the "dragoon" starship. Press Up to accelerate. When not accelerating, the ship will smoothly come to rest on the aetherial sea. Press B to fire the dragoon's flame weapon.

The demonstration stage periodically spawns debris that must be avoided or destroyed before they damage the dragoon. A single hit from the flame weapon destroys smaller objects, while the larger ones will split in two.

The two-dimensional display represents a three-dimensional volume of starlight, so your ship and any nearby objects can fly around to approach from different directions. Pay attention to debris that is floating away from you or you may find it on a collision course from a different angle.
