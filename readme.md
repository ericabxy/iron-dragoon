# Iron Dragoon

A sprite-based retro space shooter.

Two hundred years after the rise of the Roman Republic, humans sail the Sea of Stars in search of wonders and bounty. You are a veteran of the Solar-Selenian War, owner of a state-of-the-art Dragoon starship which allows you to ply your trade as an aetherial explorer and trader.

## Running the Game

_Iron Dragoon_ requires a [LibRetro](https://www.libretro.com/) front-end and the [Lutro](https://lutro.libretro.com/) core. It can be launched from a terminal using the following command.

    retroarch -L ../libretro-lutro/lutro_libretro.so .

## Playing the Game

Using a RetroPad assigned into controller port 1, press Left and Right to rotate the "dragoon" starship. Press Up to accelerate. When not accelerating, the ship will smoothly come to rest in the aetherial seas. Press B to fire the dragoon's flame weapon.

The demonstration stage periodically spawns debris that must be avoided or destroyed before they damage the dragoon. A single hit from the flame weapon destroys smaller objects, while the larger ones will split in two.

The two-dimensional display represents a three-dimensional volume of starlight, so your ship and any nearby objects can fly around to approach from different directions. Pay attention to debris that are floating away from you or you may find them on a collision course from a different angle.
